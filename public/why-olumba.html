The Problem We Solve

Design teams waste hours chasing status updates, buried in email threads, and jumping between city submittal portals. Work gets scattered across spreadsheets and chat apps—leaving no clear view of what’s approved, what’s pending, or who’s responsible.

With Olumba, design teams stay focused. Every status, submittal, and approval lives in one streamlined hub—so there’s no digging through email threads, juggling spreadsheets, or guessing who’s on point. Just forward motion.

Benefits You'll Feel This Week

  • Track every project at a glance: See what's approved, what's pending, and what's stuck in review.
  • Save hours every week: Stop hunting through portals, spreadsheets, and email threads.
  • Central collaboration: Keep architects, designers, consultants, and contractors aligned in one workspace.
  • Stay ahead of deadlines: Smart nudges and reminders surface blockers before they derail momentum.
  • Built for multi-discipline work: Capture milestones, dependencies, and submittals across architecture, interiors, MEP, landscape, and more.
  • Works with city submittals: Bring disparate portal statuses into a single, simple view.
